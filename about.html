About Us

Local IT. Real people.
No runaround.

We're 204 IT Solutions — a Winnipeg IT company built to give small businesses the reliable tech support they deserve, plus the AI and optimization tools to help them work smarter.

Built for Winnipeg's small businesses

204 IT Solutions was founded with a simple belief: small businesses deserve the same quality of IT support as large enterprises — without the enterprise price tag or the bureaucratic runaround.

We've seen too many Winnipeg businesses struggle with slow response times, confusing invoices, and tech partners who disappear after the sale. That's not us.

Our team is local, accountable, and genuinely invested in your success. When your systems are down, it's our problem too. We treat your business like it's our own.

And as technology changes, so do we. Today that means helping our clients work smarter with AI and business optimization — automating repetitive work and streamlining day-to-day operations — always with the same plain-language, no-pressure approach we built our name on.
